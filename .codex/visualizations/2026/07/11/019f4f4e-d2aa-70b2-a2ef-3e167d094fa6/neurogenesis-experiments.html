Next converged hypothesis

Renew organisms with energy they earned—not bodies the evaluator minted.

Restore a minimal energy-conserving reproduction loop, or a strictly equivalent plant-funded birth ledger. Parents must acquire ecological energy, invest it into offspring, and expose predators to renewable challenges without creating a subsidy. Only then does historical crossplay become a test of a real Red Queen rather than a finite melee.

Conservation gateEvery offspring joule must trace to parent reserves or plant-derived energy; no hidden evaluator injection.
Demographic gateOpponents must replenish through their own competence without producing runaway population growth.
Open-endedness gateLater checkpoints must expand reciprocal historical coverage, retain 8k durability, and show opponent-facing causal structure.
Failure conditionIf reproduction only yields a fixed producer–predator equilibrium or another exploitable scalar, reject it and preserve the evidence.
