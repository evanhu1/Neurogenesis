# Finding

Three experiments tested whether *perception* unlocks the intelligent-hunting loop:
- corpse channel alone (iter3): weak/negative.
- corpse channel + consume-on-kill + three-factor (iter7): mismatch, not retained.
- **live-prey channel + consume-on-kill + three-factor (iter8): matched to the
  reward, yet STILL regresses both HOLD pillars** (aeff 0.499, mi_sa 0.084) below
  champion (0.565/0.141) AND below the no-new-neurons three-factor base (0.542/0.134).

So **adding a sensory channel costs more (topology dilution: ~3 extra sensory
neurons enlarge the synapse-expression space and dilute the strongly-evolved
foraging circuitry) than the hunting signal can repay** — even when the perception
is matched to the reward and within-life learning is present. This is the same
selection-pressure law ([[mechanisms/selection-pressure-is-the-bottleneck-for-intelligence]]):
capability that isn't *paid for strongly enough* dilutes competence.

**Conclusion of the 8-iteration arc.** The best goal-aligned substrate found is
**iter6: consume-on-kill + three-factor** ([[experiments/0006-plasticity-three-factor-on-predation]])
— a predator-niche ecosystem with within-life reward-learning that RECOVERS
action_effectiveness and raises mi_sa on most seeds, narrowly short of the HOLD
gate (vs champion aeff −0.022 / mi_sa −0.007). The predation MECHANISM space is
exhausted (corpse/lethality/reliability/scarcity/reach/perception all mapped). The
**binding constraint is now the metric contract** — the predator-niche arms race IS
open-ended evolution (emergent hunting brains, rising mi_sa) but the proxies
undervalue it (prey rate structurally unreachable; action_effectiveness penalizes
predation's younger death-cohort). The high-leverage next move is the human eval
decision ([[directions/reconsider-intelligence-metric-under-predation]]); with a
metric that values mi_sa / cohort-normalizes action_effectiveness, iter6 cleanly
advances the champion to a learned-hunting ecosystem.
