# Finding

All four mechanistically-distinct softenings of the energy economy —
energy-dependent passive cost ([[experiments/0001-metabolism-homeostatic-metabolism]]),
sub-linear brain cost ([[experiments/0001-metabolism-brain-cost-discount]]), a
starvation grace window ([[experiments/0001-metabolism-soft-death-grace]]), and a
flat cost cut ([[experiments/0001-metabolism-lower-passive-cost]]) — each:

1. **lifted learning_slope** toward zero (+0.00013 to +0.00045 cross-seed), and
2. **rescued seed 2026 from extinction** (n 3→4), with **no population explosion**.

This is strong, redundant confirmation that the negative learning_slope is the
**starvation death-spiral** (dying organisms accumulate failed actions in their
final stretch → within-life success declines with age), not a defect of the
plasticity rule. Reducing terminal starvation is the dominant lever for this
surface area, and the baseline sits in a scarcity/collapse regime where there is
headroom to soften before hitting the population-explosion trap.

The differentiator among the four is the HOLD pillars: only the **homeostatic**
variant holds (improves) both action_effectiveness and mi_sa — see
[[findings/learning-gains-trade-against-action-effectiveness-in-death-pressure-regime]].
That made it the iteration-1 champion.
