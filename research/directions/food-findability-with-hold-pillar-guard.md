# Direction

All findability levers ([[experiments/0001-food-faster-regrowth]],
[[experiments/0001-food-lower-fertility-threshold]],
[[experiments/0001-food-wider-fertile-regions]]) raised plant only modestly
(0.066–0.072, far from the 0.10 target) and depressed action_effectiveness on
seed 7 — none passed the HOLD gate standalone, and food_energy cuts conflict
outright ([[mechanisms/plant-rate-tracks-metabolism-over-food-energy]]). Next:

- Stack the **gentlest** findability change (smallest action_effectiveness cost,
  likely a mild regrowth speed-up) on the **homeostatic champion** and re-gate —
  the healthier survival economy may absorb the action-quality cost.
- Investigate *why* findability depresses action_effectiveness (dilution? reduced
  selective pressure?) — if it's population-density-driven, a combined
  metabolism+food point may avoid it.
- 0.10 likely needs the combination, not any single food lever.
