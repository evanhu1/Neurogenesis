# Symbolic cognition open-endedness audit

Status: completed negative result; current search exhausted without an open-ended survivor

## Question

Does the strongest implemented symbolic controller satisfy the standing
requirement for sustained adaptive novelty and competence in the tail, or does
it reduce to bounded capability?

## Evidence considered

The audit combines:

- the multi-seed hidden-string v9, delayed-association v2, and symbolic-cognition
  v2 artifacts recorded on 2026-07-18;
- frozen-winner horizon extensions through 256 distractor or pointer steps;
- the 18-round mechanism search preserved in
  [`open-ended-search-log.md`](open-ended-search-log.md), including structural
  mutation, novelty/local competition, mixed ecology, checkpoint crossplay,
  PowerPlay, delayed memory, public task declarations, protected decoders,
  persistent energy caches, and procedural ecology;
- the fixed-machine finiteness proof and the hostile audits of the public-cache
  and procedural-ecology proposals.

## Adversarial result

The current implementation survives the bounded causal audits:

- hidden-string exact competence is 95.46%--98.97% across three seeds, while
  commit-off behavior is at eight-way positional chance;
- delayed bindings survive 256 distractor steps, while fast-state reset returns
  whole-set exact to zero;
- toy lexical top-1 is 99.35%--99.74% and closed-loop pointer exact is
  99.58%--100% on component-disjoint sealed panels.

It fails the open-endedness gate. Pointer exact is invariant through depth 256
because the learned eight-entry permutation closes into finite cycles. No new
rule, representation, behavior, or task is created as depth grows, and path
probability collapses exponentially. The delayed-memory horizon likewise
demonstrates retention of a fixed register, not growth of memory capacity or
repertoire. The benchmark portfolio therefore has capability without sustained
adaptive diversity and hits the standing prompt's **bounded novelty** failure
mode.

Literal infinite novelty cannot be demonstrated by a fixed executable with
finite state on finite hardware: for fixed configuration and seed it must
eventually halt, fail, or repeat. The relevant operational claim would instead
require a long, preregistered tail in which new adaptive structures continue to
appear faster than the evaluation horizon is extended. None of the 18 tested
families supplies that evidence. In particular, public energy-cache mechanics
passed ledger checks but left artifact semantics and payoff ownership in the
evaluator; procedural ecology passed deterministic mechanics but did not give
organisms an owned, causally grounded construction interface.

## Exact remaining gap

A materially new route must add persistent, energy-funded, recursively
composable **public niche construction** to the organism interface. Later
lineages must be able to sense, use, modify, and extend artifacts created by
earlier lineages through generic ports, under conserved energy/material and
without evaluator-computed answers. A valid experiment must require all of:

1. creator, artifact, and consumer knockouts that each destroy the claimed
   lineage-to-lineage dependency;
2. alpha-renaming, translation, and rebuild transfer to reject fixed symbol
   tables and private identifiers;
3. frozen-selection and no-payoff controls to separate adaptive novelty from
   neutral construction drift;
4. a multi-seed tail where admitted artifacts, downstream reuse depth,
   behavioral repertoire, and held-out competence continue to grow together;
5. rejection on inert artifact accumulation, finite grammar closure,
   evaluator escrow, universal-constructor scaffolding, or any plateau hidden
   by a cumulative counter.

The present crates have fast binding and a persistent private key, but no
artifact receptors/actions, conserved construction economy, generic public
ports, or lineage-owned recursive composition. Reparameterizing NEAT,
increasing generations, or extending the existing pointer horizon cannot close
that architectural gap.

## Decision

Return condition (a), a complete open-ended algorithm, is not met. Return
condition (b) is met for this search: the strongest rigorously demonstrated
result is the bounded multi-seed fast-memory controller above, and the exact
remaining failure is sustained adaptive public compositional growth. The
search is exhausted for the current symbolic interface and tested mechanism
families; this does not prove that no future architecture can satisfy the
operational gate.
# Architecture-audit note

The negative open-endedness verdict remains conservative, but the controllers
audited below were later invalidated because their task supplied the encoder,
lookup table, and side memory. Do not use this record as positive substrate
evidence or as proof of a specific next architecture.
