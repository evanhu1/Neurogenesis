# Aborted experiment

This paired 40%-versus-20% plant-density campaign was stopped immediately after
launch when the 40% baseline was rejected as ecologically oversaturated. Its
partial run directories are not evidence and must not be analyzed as completed
runs. The replacement experiment is
`artifacts/research/runs/completed/triad-pop48-12opp-food10-80g/`, using 10%
plant tiles and no 40% control.
