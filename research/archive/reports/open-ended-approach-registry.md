# Open-ended neuroevolution approach registry

Updated: 2026-07-13. Statuses are evidence states, not preferences. A blocked
family may reopen only when the proposed mechanism changes the causal source of
adaptive difficulty, representation, or selection signal; parameter tuning is
not sufficient.
